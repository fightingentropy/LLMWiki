## Antifragile / Nietzsche option-value note

> "Nietzsche says humans have no intrinsic value. But they do have the optionality/potential of becoming something. So if you kill a baby you are killing an option that has a big premium but if you kill an old bum the option premium has diminished and his potential is far less so people tend to care less."

Growth (personal, economic, biological) often accelerates under stress, not peace. Relationships that survive tension tend to deepen." — see [[Nassim_Taleb]]

## Themes across the collection

- **Action precedes feeling** — motivation, courage, and conviction follow action, not the reverse. Aim, fire, correct later.
- **Time as scarce resource** — Altman, Jobs, Kornfield, Marcus Aurelius all converge.
- **Self-betrayal as worst sin** — Dostoevsky.
- **Usefulness > correspondence truth** — a pragmatic epistemology recurs.
- **Obsession and "soul in the game"** — the soul wants to be consumed by something; craft and love share the same engine.
- **Delusion as tool** — functional self-belief running ahead of evidence.
- **Antifragility** — Nietzsche's option-value logic extended through Taleb.
- **Narrative as interface** — storytelling as compression algorithm for reality.
