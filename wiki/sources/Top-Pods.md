# Top Pods

Short list of podcast/video bookmarks the human has pinned. Sparse commentary — the file is just titles + links. Heavy skew toward masculinity discourse and celebrity conversation.

## Masculinity & the "War on Men"

- **[The performative Male epidemic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w6PNOlTVFlM)** — clip flagging the "performative male" as a cultural type. Connects to the sterile-optimization critique in [[Masculinity_and_Men]] and the secular-priesthood frame in [[Andrew_Huberman]], [[Lex_Fridman]], [[Tim_Ferriss]].
- **[The war on Men](https://youtu.be/2nDyZH0-uz0)** — framing: men as targets of systemic cultural pressure rather than beneficiaries of it. See [[Masculinity_and_Men]].

## Health & Brain

- **[How to reclaim your brain in 2026 — Huberman](https://youtu.be/YvWU4Zd-IMc)** — dated 2026 bookmark. Huberman on attention/neuroplasticity. See [[Andrew_Huberman]], [[Mental_Health]].

## Celebrity Conversations

- **[JRE — Matt Damon & Ben Affleck](https://youtu.be/AVEZBy1uAk8)** — [[Joe_Rogan]] with the two longtime Boston collaborators. Bookmarked without commentary; the JRE-as-mainstream-celebrity-room pattern matters more than the specific content.

## Tate

Two bookmarks from [[Andrew_Tate]], grouped deliberately:

- **[Tate about Fame](https://youtu.be/YjZaLqC9_z8)** — fame as cost/trap rather than reward.
- **[Tate about Fall of Empire (Second Half)](https://youtu.be/-CaV3ePx-LE)** — civilizational decline framing. See [[Political_Commentary]] on entropy/extropy and [[Masculinity_and_Men]] on decline narratives.

## Themes across the bookmarks

- **Performative vs. generative masculinity** — the "performative male epidemic" and "war on men" videos are two sides of the same diagnosis: modern masculinity has been hollowed into signalling, with no load-bearing role left.
- **Huberman as reference point** — returning to him for brain/attention protocols even while the culture around him is contested.
- **Celebrity long-form** — JRE keeps surfacing as the room where A-list actors talk at length without PR scaffolding.
- **Tate as civilizational commentator** — bookmarked not for the self-help but for the fame/empire takes.
