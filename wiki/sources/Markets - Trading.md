## Core Principle
**Truth doesn't necessarily move markets. Perception & Uncertainty/Certainty transitions do.**

Reference: https://youtu.be/rZ9rUh9WhwU?t=1876

## Key Things to Look For

### Market Drivers
- **CT Sentiment**: Crypto Twitter sentiment and positioning
- **Momentum**: Moving averages play a role; market structure is important
- **Macro**: Macro regime and policy drivers
- **Mechanical Movements**: Technical levels, liquidation cascades
- **Liquidations**: Real-time liquidation pressure and squeeze risk
- **Top Trader Positioning**: Notable traders' positions and flows
- **Catalysts**: Events and announcements with market impact
- **Stablecoin Dominance**: USDT/USDC flows and dominance shifts
- **Stablecoin Yields**: Borrow/lend rates on stables (funding proxy)
- **Funding Rates**: Perpetual futures funding (leverage indicator)
- **Options Positioning**: Implied volatility and gamma exposure
