# Medical & Health Insights

## ADHD & Circadian Rhythm
Recently reconceptualized [[ADHD]] as a circadian rhythm disorder. Implications for understanding attention and motivation through the lens of sleep-wake cycles.

Key resource: [NIH - ADHD Medications and Alertness/Motivation](https://www.nih.gov/news-events/nih-research-matters/adhd-medications-stimulate-alertness-motivation)

## Thyroid & Fluoride
**Fluoride blocks iodine at thyroid receptors.** Same receptor competition; thyroid needs iodine to produce T3/T4. Traditional fluoride toothpaste (like Colgate) delivers thyroid-suppressing chemical through highly absorbent tissue—twice daily.

**Alternative:** Hydroxyapatite toothpaste remineralizes teeth without endocrine disruption. Been used for decades; Japan's standard.

## Dopamine Synthesis
Dopamine synthesized from amino acid **Tyrosine**. First critical step requires enzyme **Tyrosine Hydroxylase**. **Sunlight is needed for this step.**

## Testosterone & Agency
Higher testosterone correlates with men doing what they know to be right instead of caving to social pressure. The declining testosterone trend may be engineered.
