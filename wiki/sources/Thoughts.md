# Thoughts & Observations

## Wealth & Status Mismatch
Insane phenomenon: 20-something autists at elite venues (F1 Paddock Club, Pit Suites) because they "hit a 50x on Fartcoin," while next to them sits someone owning water supply for Eastern Belgium. Crypto wealth vs. old money.

## One Thing Per Day
"Compared to my 20s, I'm much more at peace that I only have capacity for one optional thing really well each day. After work, relationships, dog, chores—one thing. That's okay. Better than burning out trying to do 3-4 things indefinitely."

**Acceptance prevents revenge procrastination and stress.**

## Aesthetic as Inner World Map
"Your aesthetic taste is a map of your inner world projected outward. What you're drawn to reveals which parts of your spirit are awake. Aesthetic taste is an unconscious confession."

Corollary: Elon's blind spot is beauty. Bad taste, cringe logos, bad jokes. Doesn't understand political utility of image.

## Information Density Hierarchy
"Podcasts have lowest information density of any medium ever created, but they're totally passive. Unlike reading, require zero energy expenditure. Partly why they're horrible for learning."

**Passive consumption ≠ deep understanding.**

## Autism & Integrity
"The correlation between autism and traits like honor and integrity is astronomically high relative to general population."

Autists don't do social theater; they do truth.

## Intelligence Tests
Easiest way to test someone's IQ: make generalized statement about a group ("Asian men are shorter on average"). If they respond "not ALL Asian men" or "I know a tall Asian guy"—you're dealing with genuine low IQ. They can't handle statistical abstracts. Exception ≠ refutation.

## Holidays & Freedom Taste
"I've never come back from holiday feeling relaxed and reinvigorated. I come back with taste of freedom still fresh, renewed hatred for work, strong suspicion this isn't what I should be doing."

Freedom reveals what we actually want.

## Status & Categorization
"High-level rizz move requiring intellectual dishonesty: be real-life Sorting Hat. People want identity and tribe SO badly. If you give them that (or illusion), they'll put you on pedestal."

Millennials especially love being labeled (Enneagram, Myers-Briggs, Zodiac).

## Spatial Awareness Decline
"Noticing steady increase in people seemingly unaware of existence in 3D space. Listlessly loitering in doorways, shocked when someone says 'excuse me.' Not in rude way—genuinely confused at their predicament."

Technology rewires spatial cognition.

## Physiognomy & Decision-Making
"My physiognomy has changed with decisions I make. When I started making more aggressive masculine decisions, features got sharper, frame bigger, muscle came easier from being in precarious environments. Maybe SAID principle but doesn't uncover mechanism."

Behavior shapes morphology.

## Porn & Social Positioning
Women can immediately tell if men watch porn: lack confidence around women, wandering eyes, weak authority, feminine energy, no self-control/discipline, poor spending habits. "Sin has real physical consequences."

## Work Theater Economy
"We've built entire shadow economy around simulation of work rather than work itself. Productivity-industrial complex feeds on desire to feel progress rather than make it."

**Preparatory state replaces executory state.** Always more "research" before starting. Always more "optimized" method. Always more "efficient" system. It's paralysis disguised as momentum.

## AI & Human Exceptionalism
"Illusion of human exceptionalism crumbles when you see how much intelligence is pattern recognition & predictive modeling. AI exposes creativity as remixing, insight as regurgitation, expertise as memorized heuristics."

## Opportunities & Serendipity
"If big publisher/dealer/executive suggests appointment, cancel anything planned. You may never see such window again. Opportunities don't grow on trees."

**Living in big cities invaluable.** Serendipity requires exposure to envelope of possibility.

## Gender Ideology Gap
"Delta in ideology gap between women and men gone insane."

Naval: "State can substitute for what women want from men, but can't substitute for what men want from women."

Relates to Gabor Maté's epigenetics—mother's anxiety passed to child in utero. Culture/emotional state literally changes genes.

## Small Stakes Matter
"Death lies in banality. Risk small gains for huge downside. Crash car reading notification = trade off 'read notification 3 seconds earlier' vs. 'crash, repair $1000s, hurt self/others.' Running across road stressed = trade off 'save 5 seconds' vs. death."

**Exercise care. Especially stressed.**

## Food Matrix Escape
"After breaking out of food matrix, 98% of grocery store suddenly inedible. Jarring to realize."

Processed food design exploits human vulnerability.

## Storytelling Power
"Storytelling is ultimate compression algorithm for human attention. Everything else feeds into it—data, logic, tech. If you can't wrap it in compelling narrative, nobody cares."

**Story is interface between idea and world.** Get it wrong, idea dies. Get it right, you bend steel.
