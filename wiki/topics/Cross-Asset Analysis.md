## Overview
Cross-asset analysis integrates multiple markets to identify regime, positioning, and hidden dynamics. Individual asset classes tell incomplete stories; cross-asset relationships reveal the full picture.

## Key Markets to Monitor

### Rates
- **Front-end** (2Y, 3Y): Reflects near-term policy expectations
- **Long-end** (10Y, 30Y): Growth and inflation expectations
- **Curve shape**: Inversion signals recession risk; steepening signals growth/inflation
- **Real yields**: Inflation-adjusted; drives macro regime
- **Implied policy path**: Market-priced Fed path vs. consensus

### FX (Foreign Exchange)
- **USD broad**: Dollar strength usually means risk-off/safe-haven flight
- **Key crosses**: EUR/USD (Europe growth), GBP/USD (UK policy), JPY (safe-haven)
- **EM stress signals**: EMs often first to stress in risk-off; currency weakness reveals stress
- **Carry trade unwinds**: Yen strength can force liquidations in leveraged positions

### Equities
- **Index internals**: Breadth, advance/decline ratio reveal health
- **Leadership**: Concentration (mega-cap tech) vs. dispersion (small-cap, cyclicals)
- **Sector rotation**: Cyclicals vs. defensives indicate growth expectations
- **Valuation**: P/E ratios, earnings yields reveal positioning and risk

### Credit
- **IG/HY spreads**: Wider spreads = higher risk perception, tighter = complacency
- **Refinancing stress**: Maturing debt at high rates, weak demand
- **Default risk**: Specific credits vs. market-wide stress
- **Private credit**: Less liquid, less transparent; can surprise on the downside

### Commodities
- **Oil**: Cyclical; correlated with growth and risk appetite
- **Natural gas**: Seasonal; weather, policy, and geopolitical factors
- **Metals**: Gold/silver inversely correlated with real rates and DXY
- **Food inflation**: Often leading indicator for broader inflation

### Crypto (if macro-relevant)
- **Bitcoin**: Often trades as risk asset (risk-on = up) but increasingly as macro/liquidity proxy
- **Stablecoin flows**: Money market proxy and tail-risk hedge
- **Funding rates**: Leverage indicator across crypto

## Key Relationships

### Risk-On / Risk-Off
- **Risk-on**: Equities up, credit tight, commodity price up, USD weak
- **Risk-off**: Equities down, credit wide, commodities mixed, USD strong, gold/safe havens up

### Growth Expectations
- **Growth acceleration**: Curves steep, long rates up, cyclicals outperform
- **Growth deceleration**: Curves flatten/invert, real yields down, defensives outperform

### Inflation Expectations
- **Inflation rise**: Real yields up (if growth intact), commodities up, dollar can be strong
- **Inflation fall**: Real yields down, growth-sensitive rates fall, long bonds rally

## Regime Integration
The [[Markets - Brief]] template requires coherent cross-asset calls. This means:
- Individual market views must be consistent with regime view
- Contradictory signals reveal inflections or positioning extremes
- New regimes often first appear in carry/currency markets before hitting equities

## Tail Risk Recognition
Cross-asset dislocations reveal tail risks:
- Equity-credit divergence (equities up, spreads wide) = positioning extremes
- EM currency crash + commodity collapse = emerging market crisis
- Real yield spike + equity selloff = vol regime shift
