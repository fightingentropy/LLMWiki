## Key themes

- **Aesthetic as unconscious confession.** "Your aesthetic taste is a map of your inner world projected outward. What you're drawn to reveals which parts of spirit are awake" — taste reveals character ([[Thoughts]], [[Design_and_Aesthetics]]).
- **One thing per day.** Post-20s peace with having capacity for only one optional thing well — prevents revenge procrastination and burnout ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Information density hierarchy.** Podcasts = lowest information density. Reading = higher density + active energy. Passive consumption ≠ deep understanding ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Autism ↔ integrity.** Correlation "astronomically high" relative to general population. Autists don't do social theater; they do truth ([[Thoughts]], [[Neurodiversity]]).
- **Physiognomy & decision-making.** Behavior reshapes morphology — aggressive masculine decisions change features, frame, muscle ease ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Work theater.** Shadow economy of simulated work. Preparatory state replaces executory state. Paralysis disguised as momentum ([[Thoughts]], [[Technology_Critique]]).
- **AI exposes human exceptionalism as pattern-matching.** Creativity as remixing, insight as regurgitation, expertise as memorized heuristics ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Storytelling = compression algorithm for attention.** Get narrative wrong, idea dies ([[Thoughts]], [[Philosophy_and_Meaning]]).
