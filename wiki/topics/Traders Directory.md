## Monitoring Framework

### What to Watch
- **Position updates**: Which way are they trading?
- **Conviction changes**: When do they shift views?
- **Regime calls**: Do they see regime shifts coming?
- **Flow commentary**: Stablecoin flows, liquidations, options positioning
- **Volume/support**: How many followers? Influencing retail flow?

### Uses
1. **Consensus identification**: When many traders have same view = crowded trade
2. **Dissent identification**: When traders disagree = no consensus = inflection risk
3. **Conviction measurement**: Traders' position sizing and language intensity
4. **Retail flow proxy**: Popular traders' views correlate with retail positioning
