## Stock Exchanges

### US Equities
**NYSE & NASDAQ**
- **Local (ET)**: 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM
- **UK Time**: 2:30 PM – 9:00 PM

### Asia-Pacific
**Shanghai Stock Exchange**
- **Local**: 9:30 AM – 3:00 PM
- **UK Time**: 1:30 AM – 7:00 AM

**Tokyo Stock Exchange**
- **Local**: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM
- **UK Time**: Midnight – 6:00 AM

### Europe
**Euronext**
- **Local (CET)**: 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM
- **UK Time**: 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM

## Coverage Patterns
Trading coverage by region:
- **Asian Session**: Shanghai, Tokyo (10 PM - 7 AM UK)
- **European Session**: Euronext (8 AM - 4:30 PM UK)
- **US Session**: NYSE/NASDAQ (2:30 PM - 9:00 PM UK)

## Flow Connectivity
- Asian close → European morning (morning vol in Europe)
- European close → US morning (US earnings/data reactions)
- US close → Asian morning (overnight positioning)

Key for understanding opening gaps and overnight positioning flows.
