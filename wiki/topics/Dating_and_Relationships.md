# Dating & Relationships

## Dating Culture Evolution
There's faith that dating culture is leaving "over-therapized, sterile, everything-is-toxic" era and returning to **Latin Novella energy**—drama on balconies, ringing the doorbell, getting back together a third time. 

The narrative shift: from risk-averse therapy culture back to accepting emotional intensity as part of life.

## Proximity Infatuation
"Your coworker isn't hot, they're just within 10ft of you 40hrs/week."

Context creates perceived attraction. Remove the proximity, attraction often evaporates. Recognize the illusion; don't build futures on it.

## Pre-Dating Readiness
"Do not date while: you're broke, in terrible shape, your life is chaotic. Get your life in order first."

**Fastest way to change life:** Make easy choice impossible, right choice inevitable. Burn boats.

## High-Agency Partners
Partners exhibiting high agency (can solve problems, move forward, make things happen) = foundational to relationship health.

Low-agency people stuck in victim narratives; high-agency people create possibilities.

## Love as Selection Mechanism
"You fall in love with people who make you love the person you are when around them."

Love = recognizing your best self reflected back.

## Relationship Abandon Theory
"My therapist said: 'People don't abandon people they love, they abandon people they're using.' That was all closure I needed."

Relationships end when one party becomes consumer rather than partner.

## Gender Observations
**Women:** Trained to equate existence with perception. Hence obsessive documentation (selfies, food photos, stories). Not male gaze but **female narcissism and pleasure in watching selves look hot.** Women watch mirrors; men watch porn.

**Gender ideology gap:** Delta in ideology between women and men has "gone insane." State can substitute for what women want from men, but can't provide what men want from women (purpose, challenge, genuine connection).

## Physical Warmth
"If you're physically warm guy, no woman will ever break up with you no matter what."

Warmth (literal body temperature, and emotional warmth) = powerful attachment mechanism.

## Agency in Dating
Story: "I learned something about agency on second date with now-girlfriend. I mentioned feeling cold; she about-faced into nearest hotel, said she left scarf in room last week, handed me nicest one from lost & found. That's agency."

**High-agency people create solutions on the fly.**

## Genetic Destiny
"Physiognomy is eternal. One of greatest findings of RW Twitter is that physiognomy is undeniably real. This is stuff that would take decades of travel to figure out; we share freely every day. A numinous feast."

Appearance signals health, genetics, character. It matters more than meritocratic illusions suggest.

## The Nonchalance Doctrine

Cluster of repeated aphorisms (from [[Women]]):

- "They like 'em nonchalant motherfuckers."
- "Banks give loans to people who can prove they don't need them. Girls sleep with men who don't need them."
- "Women get more attracted to you when you don't do what they want."
- "Order, don't ask, a girl to do something with you. Telling her she's coming with you to Bar A or Event X is far more invigorating to her libido than asking."
- "Lunch dates are GOAT" — an hour and a half while suited up, vibe of a guy with shit to do who is just *evaluating* her, leaving mid-warmup. ([[DrDynamis]])
- "Be the first one to reject her. Indicate she cannot be in a relationship with you. Most girls aren't used to being rejected — she'll chase to prove a point."
- "Express sexual desire, but have a level of indifference toward the outcome."

The general principle: need repels; want-from-abundance attracts. Match her enthusiasm with ⅔ of it returned (the [[Heartiste]] "golden ratio"). Never chase butterflies — "take care of your garden and they will come."

## Attraction vs Retention

Two different games:

- **Attract:** being handsome, in shape, rich.
- **Retain:** being a leader, dominance, humor, charisma, wit, certainty, ambition, intelligence, decisiveness.

(from [[Women]])

> "Looks get the woman's attention. Game keeps her around."

Corollary: the "threatening to other men" reframe of height — women aren't attracted to height per se, but to anything that makes a man threatening to other men: mass, strength, intelligence, quick wit. Height is one entry in a class. (from [[Women]])

## Hypergamy / Solipsism / Briffault's Law (the stated frame)

> Every single girl is motivated by: Hypergamy (she only dates up), Solipsism (her feelings are her reality), Briffault's Law (no value, no relationship). Understand these and her behaviour becomes predictable.

(from [[Women]]) — see [[Gender_Dynamics]] for the broader polarity context and the contradictions this frame elides.

## Breakup Asymmetry

> "The reason men take longer getting over breakups is that women spend half the relationship getting over it."

Women **front-load** detachment; men **back-load**. By the time she walks, she's already processed. (from [[Women]])

**Early-warning signs** attraction is already dying:

- "Once a woman starts acting rational and like a grownup around you, that's when you know she's not in love with you anymore."
- "When a girl is done with you she talks to you like she's in HR or something."
- "The moment she starts acting cold — remove all attention and act like she doesn't exist."

## The Forehead Kiss & Kino

Repeated in [[Women]] three times — flagged as load-bearing:

> "A woman's greatest weakness is when you kiss her on her forehead. It gives her so many different good feelings that it literally fries her brain. She feels protected, loved, cherished, not worshiped (very important) all at the same time."

The physical game layer — subtle escalation, hair-pull ponytail, eye contact, neck-kissing, touching early and often — lives under the general PUA name *Kino* (kinesthetic). "Subtle, escalated physical touch is without a doubt the most powerful way to spark attraction." Don't hide behind the bushes — declare your intentions physically and verbally.

## "Before You Get Married" Checklist

Repeated twice in [[Women]]:

> Discuss bills, parenting styles, credit, debt, religion, family dynamics, beliefs to instill in children, childhood traumas, sexual expectations, partner expectations, financial expectations, family/mental health history, bucket list, dream home, careers and education, political views.

> "Love alone is not enough."

## Tucker Max — "Love Your Work" Model

From *What Women Want*, preserved in [[Women]]: do not enter a social situation with a sexual goal; tell yourself your only goal is to meet new women and have as much fun with them as possible. Removing the sexual-pressure frame improves outcomes — Tucker reports 3× the hookups with 50% less time per woman. A behavioral analogue to "detach from outcome" in trading.

## Contradictions Across the Wiki

Flag rather than resolve (see also [[Gender_Dynamics]]):

- **Presentism vs front-loading** — "women are memoryless, only now" vs "women process breakups for half the relationship before leaving."
- **"Rational woman" coded both attractive and terminal** — slight-male-brained women are most attractive, *but* once a woman becomes rational around you she's no longer in love.
- **"Women do things for themselves"** (AJA_Cortes corrective in [[Women]]) vs the default red-pill reading that all female behaviour is strategy aimed at men.
- **Physical warmth / emotional warmth** (earlier on this page) vs **the oak-tree doctrine** of Heartiste — "be warm" vs "be immovable." Resolution depends on context; the file doesn't resolve it.
