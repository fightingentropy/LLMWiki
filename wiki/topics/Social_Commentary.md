# Social Commentary

Loose container for cross-cutting observations about contemporary society — status games, attention economies, gender dynamics, ideology drift — that don't belong to a single topic.

## In this wiki

Catch-all linked from [[Political_Commentary]] and [[Thoughts]]. Most of the material lives in [[Thoughts_and_Observations]], [[Political_Commentary]], and the culture source pages.

## Key themes

- **Gender ideology gap gone insane.** Delta between male and female ideological positions has widened dramatically. Naval: "State can substitute for what women want from men, but can't substitute for what men want from women" ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Status & categorization hunger.** "High-level rizz move requiring intellectual dishonesty: be real-life Sorting Hat. People want identity and tribe SO badly. Millennials especially love being labeled (Enneagram, Myers-Briggs, Zodiac)" ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Intelligence tests via generalization.** Responding to a statistical claim with "not ALL X" reveals inability to handle statistical abstracts. Exception ≠ refutation ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Wealth/status mismatch.** 20-something crypto autists at F1 Paddock Clubs next to old-money owners of regional water supplies — new liquidity colliding with old stock ([[Thoughts]]).
- **Work-theater economy.** Shadow economy built around simulation of work rather than work itself ([[Thoughts]], [[Technology_Critique]]).
- **Holidays reveal desire.** "I've never come back from holiday feeling relaxed. Renewed hatred for work, suspicion this isn't what I should be doing" — freedom reveals what you actually want ([[Thoughts]], [[Philosophy_and_Meaning]]).
- **Performative masculinity epidemic.** Algorithm-rewarded men as signal-emitters rather than load-bearers ([[Masculinity_and_Men]]).
- **Media as capture mechanism.** Elite-owned channels across the left/right spectrum funnel to same conclusions ([[Political_Commentary]]).
