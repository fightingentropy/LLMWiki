# ADHD

## Circadian Rhythm Reconceptualization
Recently reconceptualized as fundamentally a **circadian rhythm disorder** rather than purely an attention deficit disorder. This reframes ADHD treatment and understanding around sleep-wake cycles, chronotype, and temporal cognition.

**Key resource:** [NIH - ADHD Medications Stimulate Alertness and Motivation](https://www.nih.gov/news-events/nih-research-matters/adhd-medications-stimulate-alertness-motivation)

## Social Characteristics
People with ADHD have distinctive positive traits: **no friendship degradation mechanics.** Can text ADHD friends after months or even years of radio silence and pick conversation right back up. Remarkable consistency in maintaining connection despite temporal gaps.

## Cognitive Profile
Neurodivergent thinking—specifically autism/ADHD—is freedom from consensus-filtered reasoning. They parse information objectively ("is this true?") rather than through social safety filter ("will others be OK with me thinking this?").

This makes them **naturally free thinkers and high-agency individuals** in decision-making and belief formation.
