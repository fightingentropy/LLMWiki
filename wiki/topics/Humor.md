# Humor

Landing page for the human's saved humor archives. The wiki keeps three dedicated source pages of jokes/one-liners, plus scattered humorous bits across other pages. This page ties them together and notes the shape of the humor the human actually saves.

## Source archives

- [[Pick-up-limes]] — smaller, tighter (~1.9KB). Skewed toward **pickup-line / comeback** territory: crude dick-size comebacks, sexual innuendo, dating banter, short absurdist one-liners. Title is a wordplay on the YouTuber Pick Up Limes.
- [[Jokes]] — larger, varied (~30KB). Heavier on **Greek wordplay**, name-puns (Irish/Italian/Arab/Japanese/etc.), long-form set pieces (the element-table chemistry bit, the ancient-Greek Σ. griphos, the palm/hand romance ending in "χείρα"), and English observational/absurd one-liners. Some repeated entries across the file.
- [[Funny]] — largest (~36KB). **Link-heavy** (embedded X/Twitter URLs, YouTube timestamps, Urban Dictionary, Wikipedia); closer to **shitpost / crypto-twitter / hospitality-worker** voice than [[Jokes]]. Multilingual fragments (Greek, Albanian, Spanish, bilingual collisions). Long-form set pieces (the "Everything is priced in" wsb copypasta, Lt-Gen Carton de Wiart bio, Daft Punk/Kanye, Jocko-parody motivational routine, the "negative cash flow girlfriend" breakup, Pizza-Hut-WWF-No-Mercy time-warp). Some overlap with the other two archives.

## Recurring humor registers

Across all three archives, the same comedic modes keep showing up — useful as a taxonomy when deciding where new bits belong:

- **Greek wordplay / puns** — the dominant mode in [[Jokes]]; scattered through [[Funny]]. Puns on element names, nationalities rendered as fake names, single-word neologisms that collapse two registers (e.g. *απανθρακομακεδόνας*, *πιτσιρικαρία*, *αυτοπατίνωση*, *γλουγλουτένη*).
- **Nationality / name-pun bits** — "Ιρλανδός που...; Bart O' Butsom" format. Streaks through [[Jokes]].
- **Sexual / crude comebacks** — dick-size retorts, pickup lines, dating banter. Dominant in [[Pick-up-limes]], present in [[Jokes]] and [[Funny]].
- **Dark or nihilistic one-liners** — "I don't give a shit about losing my life, I give a shit about losing wifi"; "I can live comfortably for the rest of my life as long as I die on Sunday"; thanks-my-spine bits.
- **Long-form set pieces** — shaggy-dog stories with delayed punchlines after rows of `.` dots. The element-table finish *SiKW HORePSe KOUKLi MoU*, the "θα λύσω, μη λύσει ο Σ." griphos, the palm/hand romance ("Έμεινε χείρα"). In [[Funny]]: Lt-Gen Carton de Wiart's war-injury resume, the Neapolitan duelist who never read Dante, the "Everything is priced in" r/wsb copypasta, the Jocko-parody motivational routine, the "negative cash flow girlfriend" breakup.
- **Observational absurd** — Mitch-Hedberg-shaped bits (kid at the airport as luggage, shark triathlon, ants dressed as rice robbing a restaurant, Kasparov and the checkered tablecloth).
- **Meta / format jokes** — the Roman-numeral "I M LIVID" reveal; the SVU parody transposed to lawn maintenance; Giordano Pietrosono's 12 rules to be an Italian where rules 1/3/4/5/11 are all variants of "shut up".
- **Crypto / markets shitpost** — mostly a [[Funny]] register: "chronically on-chain", Voldemort-horcrux hardware-wallet brag, "Rather be bullish and wrong than bearish and gay", the wsb "Everything is priced in" copypasta, "algorithmic laddered delta neutral gamma capture strat" parody. Cross-refs [[Crypto Trading]] and [[Trading Philosophy]].
- **Hospitality / restaurant-worker voice** — almost exclusively [[Funny]]: complaints about restaurant urgency, free-food smuggling, the "armwrestling customers" bit, observations about customers bringing their own water and tea bags. The human has (or had) restaurant work in their background; this is first-person material, not curated.
- **Link-and-URL bookmarks** — [[Funny]]'s defining feature. ~30 embedded X/Twitter URLs and YouTube timestamps, plus Urban Dictionary and Wikipedia links. Treated as mnemonics for something seen online rather than jokes lifted as text.

## Tonal notes

- Content is deliberately **crude, sexual, and dark-comedic** in places, with occasional slur-laden lines. The schema says preserve voice and not sanitize — all three archives are stored verbatim.
- Greek, English and Albanian bits coexist without translation; some jokes only work in Greek (wordplay), some only in English, a handful ride on bilingual collisions (*Japan ενδεχόμενο*, *ΚΤΕΛ Αβίβ*, *Tattoo Καίσαρος*, *φωτόσπαθο μες στο κλαμπ*).
- The human saves both **punchline jokes** (setup → payoff) and **vibe fragments** ("Brutal hippo attack", "Sound check", "Will smith husband hair lost", "Hideous Nigger", "Wired headphones.") — the latter presumably as mnemonics for something seen online.
- **Cross-archive repeats**: a handful of lines appear in two or three of the archives (e.g. "math test / boyfriend" cheating line, "Θα χέσω με καισαρική", "blowjob voice" Urban Dictionary link, "I'm very vulnerable rn", "Έλα να μου ξεβουλώσεις τ'αρχίδια"). Confirms these are personal re-saves, not a deduplicated collection.
