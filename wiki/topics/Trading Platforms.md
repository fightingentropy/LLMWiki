## Overview
Landscape of trading platforms used for markets execution and analysis.

## Platforms by Type

### Crypto Derivatives
- **[[Hyperliquid]]**: Decentralized perpetual futures
  - Latency monitoring: https://hyperlatency.glassnode.com/
  - On-chain explorer: https://hyperevmscan.io/

### Traditional Derivatives
- **[[CME]]**: Centralized commodities and equity index futures
  - Largest derivatives exchange globally
  - Primary price discovery for commodities, equities, rates

### Analytics & Monitoring
- **Qwantify**: Market flow analysis
  - URL: https://www.qwantify.io/app/analytics/market-flow
- **CoinGlass**: Liquidations and positioning
  - URL: https://www.coinglass.com/pro/futures/LiquidationMap
- **Loris Tools**: Real-time liquidations
  - URL: https://loris.tools/liquidations

## Platform Selection Criteria
- **Liquidity**: Can you get your size done?
- **Execution**: What's the slippage?
- **Fees**: Taker/maker dynamics, rebates
- **Leverage**: How much can you use?
- **Stability**: Risk of platform failures
- **Regulatory**: Jurisdiction and oversight
