## Overview
Global macro trading focused on regime analysis, cross-asset relationships, and inflection points.

## Core Approach
Based on [[Stanley Druckenmiller]] methodology:
- **Market regime assessment** is primary
- **Cross-asset integration** reveals hidden dynamics
- **Positioning and flow** context drives returns
- **High-conviction, signal-driven** decision-making
- **Risk-centric** with specific, observable triggers

## Key Insight
**Truth doesn't necessarily move markets. Perception & Uncertainty/Certainty transitions do.**

This fundamental principle drives macro trading: what matters is not objective reality but how market participants interpret it. Transitions from uncertainty to certainty (or vice versa) create inflection points.

## Analysis Framework

### Market Regime
- **Growth/Inflation Regime**: Disinflationary, reacceleration, or stag risk
- **Liquidity Backdrop**: Fed policy, Treasury issuance, QT/QE flows
- **Volatility Regime**: Suppressed, transitioning, or unstable

### Cross-Asset Integration
- Rates (front-end, long-end, curve, real yields)
- FX (USD broad, key crosses, EM stress)
- Equities (breadth, leadership, cyclicals vs defensives)
- Credit (spreads, refinancing stress)
- Commodities (oil, gas, metals implications)
- Crypto (as macro/liquidity proxy)

### Positioning & Flow
- Consensus crowded trades
- Dealer/gamma/CTA/systematic flow context
- Sentiment extremes and squeeze opportunities
