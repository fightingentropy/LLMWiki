# DrDynamis

Pseudonymous X/Twitter voice quoted extensively throughout the [[Women]] source. Appears under a cluster of overlapping handles — **@DrDynamis, @DrdynamisA, @dynamis_dr, @ArcadianRythms** — with the same voice and recurring signature lines. Treated as a single authorial entity here.

## Voice & Aesthetic

- **Mediterranean-stoic moralist.** Aesthetic sensibility anchored in the Arab/Greek/Levantine world — shisha, olive trees, Mediterranean languages, artillery barrages, South Park, Sam Cooke, the A10 Warthog, "the entire country of Japan."
- **Anti-nerd, anti-soy, anti-fetish, anti-porn.** Coded opposition to the "over-self-controlled respectable man" as a failure mode.
- **Pro-prayer, pro-God, pro-lifting, pro-suit.** "Lifting and prayer" as the highest-ROI activities in life.
- **Anti-tradcon optimization culture.** Dismisses the "Panasonic-air-purifier / 25-supplements / shit-transplant" bodybuilder as getting no pussy and being "z most boring dude." Parallel to the [[Masculinity_and_Men]] critique of sterile optimizers.
- **Pro-Adventure / Alchemy work, anti-pleb hard work.** "Hard work is pleb shit. Only hard work men truly enjoy is (1) Hard work that feels like Adventure (biz/ranching) or (2) Hard work that feels like Alchemy (writing)."

## Core Positions (from [[Women]])

### On fear and confrontation
> "Number 1 killer of men isn't seed oils — but a fear of confrontation. Can't even get the bartender's attention when she skips you for the 8th time. Wait like a little doggie. That weakness bleeds into everything in life."

### On being too perfect
> "There are 2 problems with being overly self-controlled and respectable. 1) It makes it very hard to read people — when people really respect you, they never show you their bad side. 2) The second you make one big blunder after years of perfection, you're done. Never seem too perfect. Publicly lash out, say crazy shit, get sloppy drunk and take off your shirt every once in a while."

### On the hyper-successful
> "A lot of the hyper-successful people I was rubbing shoulders with are dumb as hell. Now I'm starting to notice that they tend to act dumb on purpose. Everyone from welfare class to upper-middle class tries to 'behave up'. Paris Hilton isn't a bimbo."

### On ennui misnamed as depression
> "80% of dudes DMing me are complaining about feeling 'stuck' or depressed. What you dudes are feeling is not depression. It is called ENNUI — a feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from a lack of occupation and excitement."

### On respect vs adoration
> "Why do soy dudes care about being liked? Walk up to any sane dude you slightly know and tell him 'I don't like you but I do respect you' — he'll shake your hand and buy you a beer. Say 'I like you but I don't respect you' and you'll have made an enemy for life. Respect > Adoration for men."

### On testing friendship
> "Tell him about some misfortune that happened to you and observe his face. At that exact moment, his face will either reveal (1) Sincere sympathy, or (2) The passing hint of a smile."

### On fetish
> "Any sort of 'fetish' is symptomatic of something broken in the human soul. No need for a physically and mentally robust person to engage in kinks. Too many dorks out there 'dominating' their fat gfs with BDSM yet can't muster the balls to ask their boss for a raise."

### On the emotional umbilical cord
> "Important to always respect and cherish your parents without seeking their approval. Part of the reason lots of dudes struggle with relationships is because they've never cut the emotional umbilical cord with their mother. Looking for comfy mommy instead of a chick."

### On pegging the cowardice/arrogance failure mode
> "Worst character combination in men is probably cowardice + arrogance. Aka nerds. Contrary to popular belief, most nerds are actually extremely arrogant but too pussy to behave unabashedly so. End up passive aggressive instead."

### On stinginess
> "Never met a stingy dude I wanna be friends with. Penny pinching is inherently feminine in nature. Misers never make it far. Their spirit is that of a nervous squirrel hoarding nuts for winter."

### On stress, not sodium
> "Stress is the biggest culprit when it comes to heart disease. Not sodium. Biggest blind spot in modern medicine isn't gut health and nutrition — it's the total disregard towards long-term emotional states. Not natural to be on edge 265 days a year for 20 years."

### On 1st dates
> "Dinner and drink 1st dates aren't ideal. Lunch dates = GOAT. You've got an hour and a half while being all suited up, meeting a chick while she can't even look her best. Giving off the vibe of a dude who has shit to do and is just 'evaluating' her, leaving mid-warmup. Its over."

### On fashion
> "Male style should include: (1) **Order** — proper fit, clean, ironed, non-vulgar; something an army officer could wear. (2) **Quality** — well-made natural items: cotton, wool, leather, silk, metals. (3) **Fuck you** — touch of rebellion: 4 buttons unbuttoned, messy hair, sleeves pulled up."

### On Finasteride
> "Finasteride is extremely effective but a VERY dangerous drug. Significant minority of dudes have become permanently impotent from 5a-reductase inhibitors. Better to just get a hair transplant — Constantinople for 5k all included."

### On hype foods
> "Unless you really like the taste, nutritionally there's nothing you get from (1) Blueberries (2) Salmon that you can't get from (1) Oranges (2) Sardines."

### On average privilege
> "'Average Privilege' is a thing. That's why you should never listen to advice from someone with natural average privilege. They can live with that. The 7–15% 'minorities of the soul' can't function at the mean. Life entirely breaks apart without glory. If they fail to become great, they do not become average. They retreat into themselves and do the bare minimum to survive, or they off themselves."

### On early 30s
> "Early 30s is peak existence for a man. Younger dudes look up to you. 60-year-old men take you seriously. Can pull 19-year-old chicks easily. Could walk around with 40-year-old heiresses if you choose to. You do NOT want to settle down at 24."

### On timeless things
> "Timeless stuff: God. Suits. Chicks in summer dresses. Swords. The A10 Warthog. Sam Cooke. Barbequed meat / raw fish. Olive trees. South Park. Shisha (Hookah). Arabic, Greek & Farsi. Artillery Barrages. The entire country of Japan. Everything else is at least 10% gay."

### On final accounting
> "90% of us are gonna be rotting in a grave in 50 years. Only things that matter are: (1) Did you get right with God? (2) Did you live like a G? (3) Did you leave an impact on this fallen earth? Everything else means nothing."

### On inputs with the highest ROI
> "Nothing comes close pound for pound & time-invested-vs-output-gained wise than (1) Lifting (2) Prayer. For just 5 hours of lifting a week you can change your entire existential experience on earth. Just 20 minutes of true prayer a day brings in more luck it feels like cheating."

### On saying things out loud
> "A church father once told my father not to speak out his plans out loud. At least as little as possible. Because the devil cannot hear your thoughts. Only your words."

### On what you look like in the dark
> "Most think about how they look outside. Few think about how they look in the dark when nobody is watching. How do you act, look and behave with no one except God watching you? Sort that out. Then you got life sorted out."
