# Charles Bukowski

American poet and novelist (1920–1994). Known for a terse, working-class voice and advice that sounds crude or reductive but is often operationally sound.

## Why he appears here

A bookmarked YouTube clip (see [[Youtube]]) frames his advice as "funny but might work for a lot of people" — the human's take is that the surface-level simplicity hides a workable rule-of-thumb for many.

## Connection to other themes

- **Action over overthinking** — fits the [[Self_Improvement]] frame of imperfect action > perfect planning.
- **Find what you love and let it kill you** — the commonly attributed Bukowski posture lines up with the [[Philosophy_and_Meaning]] "do epic shit" framing: life organized around doing the thing, not optimizing around it.
