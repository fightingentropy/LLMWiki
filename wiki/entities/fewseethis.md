Twitter: @fewseethis. Crypto-twitter commentator quoted in [[CT]]. Known in the archive for the framing that cascades are a secondary effect of price moves in levered environments, not the primary cause.

## Line in the source

> "the simple truth is that cascades occur as a RESULT of prices going down within a levered environment. in other words a cascade is a secondary effect. then — outside of a mechanical bounce — prices can resume wherever they were going in the first place: up or down." (from [[CT]])
