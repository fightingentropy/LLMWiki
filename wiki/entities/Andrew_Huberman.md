# Andrew Huberman

## Profile
Neuroscientist and podcaster known for optimized lifestyle protocols and dopamine-focused content. @hubermanlab.

## Contributions to Popular Knowledge
- Magnesium supplementation protocols (Threonate/Bisglycinate, 30-60min before bed)
- Dopamine regulation and cold exposure benefits
- Sleep and circadian rhythm optimization
- Exercise neuroscience

## Critical Perspective
Subject of cultural critique regarding "sterile optimization" culture:

"Andrew Huberman: the neuroscience demigod whose dopamine maps rarely intersect with intimacy or fatherhood. He's a cult leader of sterile optimization. Selling surrogate masculinity unmoored from duty, generational continuity, or real-world consequences."

"Do you want to follow men who optimize their testosterone—or men who use it to build a future?"

**The critique:** High-performance optimization content maximizes engagement and loyalty, but frames masculine development as separation from family-building, legacy-creation, and traditional responsibilities.

## Paradox
Simultaneously: 
- Credible neuroscience communicator improving people's health
- Emblematic of algorithm-rewarded performance masculinity disconnected from relational/generational responsibility

Also notable: Reported to date multiple women simultaneously at age 48, with optimization-enabled lifestyle enabling this.
