## Overview
Twitter: @CryptoCred. Crypto trader and educator; widely cited for trading psychology, bag-holding heuristics, and self-aware CT commentary.

## Recurring Themes (from [[CT]])

### Bag-holder test
> "'If I didn't have a position, would I want the exposure I have now?' Yes → Cool, you're fine. No → Do something about it. Active positions cost mental capital, which is a scarce resource." (from [[CT]], cited in [[Trading_Psychology]])

### First-cycle participants
Open-minded optimism makes first-cyclers money, and loses it for them at the top.
> "Navigating cycles requires switching between childlike idealism and sagely cynicism" (from [[CT]])

### Bear market PTSD
> "One argument for trading less actively when markets suck is that you don't learn habits that cost you money in bull markets" (from [[CT]])

### Microstructure bloat / self-parody
> "Before: price go down bad. Now: perp hedges getting unwound into spot sells but Bitfinex CVD is diverging and stablecoin smart money ratio is up while FTX TWAP absorbing …" (from [[CT]])

### FTX "evil eye" / flex-culture

### CT content genres

### Education promise
> "Will I tell you when to buy and sell? No. Will I tell you when I'm buying and selling? No. But will I provide you with the necessary tools and techniques so that you can intelligently trade yourselves? Also no." (from [[CT]])
