## Overview
Legendary macro investor whose daily briefing methodology forms the template for [[Markets - Brief]].

## Druckenmiller-Style Approach
The daily market overview template is designed in his style:
- **Direct, high-conviction, PM-ready** analysis
- Focus on market regime and shifts
- Cross-asset integration (rates, FX, equities, credit, commodities, crypto)
- Emphasis on positioning and flow context
- Risk-centric thinking with specific triggers
- Signal over narrative

## Key Principles
- Truth doesn't necessarily move markets; perception and certainty transitions do
- Market regime assessment is primary
- Cross-asset correlations reveal hidden dynamics
- Positioning extremes create squeeze opportunities
- Observable confirmation signals matter more than predictions
