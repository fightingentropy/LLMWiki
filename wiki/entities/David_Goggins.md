# David Goggins

Former US Navy SEAL, ultramarathon runner, and author of *Can't Hurt Me* and *Never Finished*. Appears in the wiki as the canonical voice of discipline, suffering-as-fuel, and refusing comfort.

## Lines in the source

> "You are in danger of living a life so comfortable and soft, that you will die without even realizing your true potential." (from [[Quotes]], cited in [[Self_Improvement]])

> "If you could walk on water, the haters will say it's because you can't swim." (from [[Quotes]])

Goggins sits alongside Musashi, Napoleon and [[Peter_Thiel]] in the Action/Agency/Courage cluster of [[Quotes]] — comfort as the failure mode that swallows potential.
