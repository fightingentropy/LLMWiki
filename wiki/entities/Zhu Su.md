# Zhu Su

Twitter: @zhusu. Co-founder of Three Arrows Capital (3AC), the crypto hedge fund that collapsed in 2022 during the LUNA/Celsius cascade. Subsequently launched OPNX (a claims-trading exchange) with his 3AC co-founder Kyle Davies. High-profile and controversial figure in crypto; his takes remain widely read.

- [[X-Bookmarks]] — entry #890, on the dangers of selling the top
- [[Crypto Trading]] — cited directly on top-selling psychology

## Line in the source

> "In my experience, I've found that it is often more dangerous to sell the top than to sell on the way down. This is because the euphoria associated with selling the top can lead to early rebidding as well as a sense of overconfidence." (from [[X-Bookmarks]] #890)

The point pairs with [[Pentosh1]]'s "take life-changing money off the table" discipline — both are about exit psychology, but Zhu frames the specific failure mode (euphoric top-sell → premature re-entry).
