# Lee Kuan Yew

## Profile
Founding father and first Prime Minister of Singapore. Known for pragmatic governance and technological advancement focus.

## Key Quote on Air Conditioning & Civilization
"Air conditioning was a most important invention for us, perhaps one of the signal inventions of history. It changed the nature of civilization by making development possible in the tropics.

Without air conditioning you can work only in the cool early-morning hours or at dusk. The first thing I did upon becoming prime minister was to install air conditioners in buildings where the civil service worked. This was key to public efficiency."

## Significance
Lee Kuan Yew recognized that **climate infrastructure directly determines cognitive capacity and economic output**.

In tropical regions, temperature management = foundational public health and economic policy.

This insight preceded modern neuroscience confirming temperature-cognition relationship by decades.
