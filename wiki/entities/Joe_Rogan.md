# Joe Rogan

## Profile
Comedian, commentator, and host of The Joe Rogan Experience (JRE) — the dominant long-form podcast in Western media. Interviews span scientists, fighters, comedians, politicians, and A-list actors.

## Role in the Media Stack
JRE functions as one of the few remaining rooms where mainstream celebrities sit for 2-3 hours unscripted. That length alone is load-bearing — it collapses the PR-scaffolded interview format the legacy press relies on.
