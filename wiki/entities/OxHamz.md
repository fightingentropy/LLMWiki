# OxHamz

Twitter: @OxHamz (also written 0xhamz). Crypto commentator quoted in [[CT]]. Known in the archive for a flow-based framework on crypto-equity correlation — equities can be bid by rebalancing inflows while crypto relies on new entrants, producing apparent decorrelation without any forward-looking information content.

> "Correlation discussion needs a flow framework. Equities can get bid from rebalancing inflows from assets like bonds (eg month end pension rebalance). Crypto relies on new entrants. Therefore equities can go up while crypto is flat/down." (from [[CT]])
