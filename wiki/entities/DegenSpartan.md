## Overview
Twitter: @DegenSpartan. Long-running bearish/cynical voice on crypto. Frames crypto as a bet on societal degradation rather than technological progress.

## Recurring Themes (from [[CT]])

### Two framings of crypto
> "you think crypto is a bet on human ingenuity, financial self sovereignty and the natural progression of civilization. i think crypto is a bet on degradation of society, failure of trust and trad economies, forced speculation, desperation and human greed — we are not the same" (from [[CT]])

### Tokens selling tokens
> "imagine if all the protocols sell each others tokens (and their own tokens) and in the end its just retail bagholding + funds with a position too large that they can never exit" (from [[CT]])

### Blandification of the world
> "the world slowly creeps to become more cold, neutral and banal" (from [[CT]])
