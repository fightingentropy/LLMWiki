## Overview
Prominent crypto and macro trader known for thoughtful analysis and positioning calls. Long-form essayist on CT. Has hosted *UpOnly* podcast.

## Recurring Themes (from [[CT]])

### Grow fast, protect slow
Attributed to "someone once told Cobie" and repeated by him:
> "you act too quickly when you are trying to grow capital, and act too slowly when you are trying to protect capital" (from [[CT]], cited in [[Trading_Psychology]])

### Supercycle / Everything Bubble
> "A decade long supercycle bubble of speculative cryptocoins as a siren for the market top of the multi-decade Everything Bubble" (from [[CT]])

### Elites and inevitability
> "The billionaires multiplied wealth selling sugar to children … They tried to prevent crypto … We got money too now. Are you scared?" (from [[CT]])

### Low-conviction money bids new projects
Long passage on fomo-capitulating boomer LPs bidding outerspace metaverse hotels as entry to crypto; "they recursively programmed themselves to lose" (from [[CT]]).

### Jubilee pushback
Replying to @twobitidiot's "let investment banks die":
> "It's likely they have carefully woven themselves nicely into the lives of regular people, probably via pensions or something" (from [[CT]])

### Grateful gulf
Reflection on deca-billion crypto peers vs old co-workers stuck between paychecks — "money has meaning again" (from [[CT]]).

### "Sit on my ass" realization
Replied to Lord Ashdrake's sit-on-ass framing: "took me like 8 years to learn this toh" (from [[CT]]).

### Decentralized machines
Replying to @Chubbicorn219 on an orderly crypto crash:
> "Everyone died, but the machines did their jobs perfectly. A beautiful world." (from [[CT]])
