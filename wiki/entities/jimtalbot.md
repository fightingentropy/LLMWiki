## Overview
Twitter: @jimtalbot. Crypto trader with a mix of mechanical microstructure posts and esoteric/philosophical takes.

## Quotes (from [[CT]])

### Range → expansion mechanic
> "you build range to generate enough open interest to cause expansion out of range when it hits the stop losses of the OI that has been built up causing forced selling. The longer the range the bigger the expansion" (from [[CT]])

### Range vs trend
> "sideways, you interact as far away from average price as possible, in trend you interact with as close to average price as possible" (from [[CT]])

### Straussian esotericism
> "The Straussian distinction between exoteric and esoteric communication is a profound culture-neutral phenomenon. Wisdom can in fact never be gained while in the herd; it can only be found by deciphering the soft footprints of the mystics" (from [[CT]])

### Keynesian EMH critique
> "Keynesian professors will teach you about efficient markets hypothesis and a week later explain why govt agents sitting on huge unauditable losses on bond trades is part of the natural functioning of a modern economy" (from [[CT]])
