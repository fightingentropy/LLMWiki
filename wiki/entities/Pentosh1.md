## Overview
Twitter: @Pentosh1. Long-tenure crypto trader; 2,500+ consecutive trading days self-reported. Repeatedly emphasizes taking life-changing money off the table and fearing greed in back-half of bull cycles.

## Recurring Themes (from [[CT]])

### Take money off the table
> "If you make life changing money. Take it out of the market. Many of you will make life changing money, but few will keep it … One day the music stops, and you won't have a chair." (from [[CT]], cited in [[Trading_Psychology]])

### "Never yours"
> "The only money that is yours is what you take out into the real world. Making money is easy. Keeping money isn't." (from [[CT]])

### Dilution kills bulls
> "Once there are too many bulls, there's no-one left to buy. Then the market dies from dilution … The end simply comes when the dilution exceeds the amount of capital inflows." (from [[CT]])

### Market forces participation
> "The markets job is to force participation on your end. it moves up to force you to want to buy and down to force you to want to sell … it moves when people are offsides, forcing their participation" (from [[CT]])

### Back-half of bull = fear
> "The largest gains come in the back half of the bull run as greed completely takes over. The only way to survive is fear at that point." (from [[CT]])
