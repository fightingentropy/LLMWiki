# Alex Hormozi

Entrepreneur and business author (Acquisition.com, "$100M Offers", "$100M Leads"). Appears in the wiki as a podcast guest whose framing on **imperfect action** the human specifically bookmarked to study.

## Imperfect Action

On the [[Chris_Williamson]] podcast (video `Gk8EGWoGnEQ`, [timestamp](https://youtu.be/Gk8EGWoGnEQ?t=3590)), Hormozi's argument the human flagged from [[Go Do]]: execution beats planning, and perfect-looking plans are usually procrastination dressed as preparation. Matches the existing "Imperfect Action > Perfect Planning" section in [[Self_Improvement]] — "aim, fire, correct later."

The same video is the source of three other bookmarks already filed in [[Youtube]] (views on happiness, the only way is through, "my goal is to do epic shit"). It appears to be a load-bearing reference for the human's action-over-optimization stance.
