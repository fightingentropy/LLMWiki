## Recurring Themes (from [[CT]])

### Crypto as engaging money game
> "Crypto will continue to expand because it is vastly better than both casinos and other trading markets in terms of: 1. volatility 2. variety 3. complexity 4. social component 5. higher odds of developing an edge. It is simply the most engaging multi player money game" (from [[CT]])

### PvP vs PvE heuristics
> "There are certain heuristics that work in PvP market conditions that don't apply as much in PvE market conditions … fading high funding/up only price action … In bull markets funding can stay higher than shorts can remain solvent. The second is the need to rotate … To outperform like retail you need to think like retail" (from [[CT]])
