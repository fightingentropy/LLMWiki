# Peter Thiel

German-American investor and entrepreneur (b. 1967). Co-founder of PayPal and Palantir, first outside investor in Facebook, author of *Zero to One*. Known for contrarian-question framing ("what important truth do very few people agree with you on?") and for the claim that courage is scarcer than genius.
