# Wiki Overview

A personal knowledge wiki spanning self-improvement, markets, electrics, and culture. Bootstrapped from an existing Obsidian vault on 2026-04-12.

## Domains

### Personal & Psychology
The most thematically rich domain. Centres on a recurring tension between **optimization culture** (biohacking, productivity, self-improvement) and **meaning-making** (philosophy, legacy, lived experience). Key figures: [[Andrew_Huberman]], [[Nassim_Taleb]], [[Curtis_Yarvin]], [[Jeff_Bezos]]. Core themes include [[ADHD]] as an attention allocation difference rather than deficit, [[Philosophy_and_Meaning]] (entropy vs extropy, compounding small decisions), and [[Self_Improvement]] with a critical eye toward sterile optimization disconnected from real life.

### Health & Body
Practical optimization notes. [[Supplements]] (creatine, magnesium, zinc, vitamin D), [[Body_Optimization]] protocols, [[Fitness]] approaches. [[Medical]] notes on fluoride/iodine thyroid competition, testosterone/dopamine links. Currently data-light — mostly bookmarked claims, few with citations.

### Markets & Trading
The most structured domain. Built around a [[Trading Philosophy]] that "truth doesn't move markets — perception does." Includes a complete [[Macro Trading]] framework with a Druckenmiller-style daily brief template, detailed [[Commodities]] breakdowns ([[Oil]], [[Natural Gas]], [[Gold]], [[Silver]]), [[Crypto Trading]] mechanics ([[Funding Rates]], [[Liquidations]], [[Leverage]]), and a [[Traders Directory]] of 19+ accounts to follow. [[Exchange Hours]] and [[Exchange Microstructure]] pages serve as quick-reference.

### Electrics
Technical study notes for electrical certification. Comprehensive coverage of [[Electrical Safety]], [[Building Regulations]] (Part P), [[Safe Isolation]] procedures, [[Continuity Testing]], [[In-Service Testing]] (PAT), and the [[EIC and MEIWC]] certification framework. All formulas and regulation numbers preserved. References [[BS 7671]] and [[GN3 Guidance Note 3]].

### Culture & Politics
Observations on society, dating dynamics, aesthetics, and political commentary. Less structured than other domains — more a collection of takes and frameworks than a developed thesis. [[Political_Commentary]] spans UK wage compression, immigration, and US policy analysis. [[Dating_and_Relationships]] captures behavioral observations. [[Design_and_Aesthetics]] is mostly a tool bookmark collection.

### Dev & Design
Lightweight — mostly link collections. [[Developer_Resources]] and [[Design_and_Aesthetics]] catalogue tools and references. [[Tools]] covers setup preferences (Claude Code, Obsidian, Helium).

## Gaps and Opportunities

- **Health claims lack sources.** Most supplement and optimization notes are unsourced assertions. Worth finding papers or Huberman episode links to back them up.
- **Culture domain is thin.** Lots of scattered observations but no synthesis pages pulling themes together.
- **No analyses yet.** The `wiki/analyses/` directory is empty — no comparisons, timelines, or synthesized arguments have been filed. This will grow as queries get answered and filed.
- **Dev domain is just bookmarks.** Could be enriched as projects and tools are actually used.
- **Cross-domain connections are sparse.** Trading psychology connects to personal psychology; health optimization connects to trading performance. These links could be made more explicit.
